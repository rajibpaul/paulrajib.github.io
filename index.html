About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Korea University. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Ajou University, South Korea. Previously, I worked as a Standardization Engineer, contributing to wireless standards development, and served as an Assistant Professor at Ajou University for more than five years.

My research focuses on cognitive radio networks, AI-driven wireless communication, edge computing, and resource allocation in next-generation wireless systems (5G/6G). I specialize in developing Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL), Federated Learning (FL), and optimization algorithms for efficient spectrum management, intelligent edge computing, and adaptive resource allocation in wireless networks.
