Hello!

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Software and and Computer Engineering in Ajou University. I have acomplished my Master and PhD degree from Ajou University in the year 2012 and 2017, respectively. My research focus is comprised of Wireless Communications, Resource Management, D2D Communications, Vehicular Netwokrs, and Machine Learning.
